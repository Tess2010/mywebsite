Welcome to the start of my journey to become a full stack developer. On this site I will share some of my coding progress, favorite crafts, books, music, recipes and personal insights


My Journey

I never thought at the age of 57, I would be starting a new career within the Tech Community. I'm hoping that this website will inspire others to realize that it's never "too late" to pursure your goals and dreams. This site will be a space for me to document my growth, share some stories and maybe connect with others who share the same path

How it started

November of 2022, I was laid-off as an Administrative Assistant. I struggled with self-doubt and a lot of uncertainty. In May of 2024 I was given the opportunity to participate in a 2 year program, The Matrix Code.

During my learning journey, I've encountered some challenges, such as adapting to a new way of thinking, an example of this would be when I would try to write the code without actually breaking down what was being asked instead of designing a solution first; grasping concepts such as methods/functions and objects; and debugging - trying to find errors can be frustrating, when you don't have the experience. Each challenge I faced eventually became a lesson and I'm excited about the progress I've made thus far.

What's next

My current focus right now is mastering HTML and CSS and working on this site . I'm looking forward to sharing my progress, projects and insights with you.

"Every failure is a win, because if you've learned from your failure, it has now become a lesson."

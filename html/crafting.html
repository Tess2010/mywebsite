Recipes I Love

I'm not much of a baker, I leave that to my sister and daughters and I just enjoy the results. I prefer cooking meals or making appetizers, especially when we have family get togethers or "girl's" night. I thought I'd share a few of my go to recipes when I'm looking to makes something simple but flavorful. Please remember that these recipes are made to suit my tastes, you may want to adjust the ingredients or add a little something extra for yourself. If you happen to try one, please leave me a comment and let me know.
  1. Easy Cheezy Cheeseball
      Ingredients:
      • 3 cups of grated cheddar cheese
      • 1 small minced onion
      • 1/4 cup of sour cream
      • 1 pkg of cream cheese (if not using Flakes of turkey, I like using herb & garlic cream cheese)
      • Optional - 1 can of Flakes of turkey (crushed)
      • Optional - chopped walnuts
      Directions:
      • Blend sour cream and cream cheese together, once smooth, add remaining ingredients minus chopped walnuts.
      • Mix thoroughly, shape into balls and wrap with saran wrap. Chill until ready to use.
      • Roll in chopped walnuts before serving. Can be made ahead of time and frozen for up to 4 weeks in the freezer.
      • If freezing, do not roll in nuts. Let it unthaw in fridge overnight before serving.

      Bacon Ranch Variation: Instead of minced onion & Flakes of Turkey, add 3-4 tbsp of chopped green onion, a pkg of ranch dressing mix and crumbled bacon.

  2. Crock Pot Brown Sugar & Garlic Chicken
      Ingredients:
      • 5 chicken thighs
      • 2-3 tbsp of minced garlic (6-8 medium cloves)
      • 1/2 tsp salt
      • 1/4 tsp pepper
      • 1/3 cup of brown sugar
      Directions:
      • Add chicken, salt, pepper & garlic to crockpot.
      • Mix everything together and place skin side up. Sprinkle brown sugar over top
      • Cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4 hours

  3. Easy Peasy Chicken Pot Pie
      Ingredients:
      • 2 pack pre-made pie crust
      • 1 can each cream of mushroom & cream of chicken
      • 2 cups of chopped rotisserie chicken
      • 1/2 of a small bag of frozen vegetables
      • splash of milk
      • salt,pepper, garlic & thyme - to taste preference
      • melted butter for pie pan & top of pie
      • Optional - potatoes (uncooked 1/2 inch cubed or pre cook 5-7 minutes and cube)
      • flour for dusting pan
      Directions:
      • Preheat oven to 375 degrees
      • Mix all ingredients together
      • Butter & lightly dust pie pan with flour
      • Lay 1 pie crust in pan and pour in mixture
      • Top with second pie crust and seal
      • Bake for 40 mins, brush top with melted butter and broil for 2 mins.

      Variations:
      • Beef - Use 3 - 4 cups of gravy to replace soup & milk. Pre-cooked ground beef or beef can be used.
      • Turkey - Replace chicken with turkey and summer savoury to taste.
  4. Hurry Curry Chicken
